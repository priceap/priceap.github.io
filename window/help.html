Help:



This icon marks your location on the map. Touch the icon to open a dialog for recording your thoughts that you wish to associate with this location. The recording will appear on the map for others to listen.



This icon marks audio recordings that conference participants have created at its location. Touch the icon to listen to the audio.



The window icons indicate the location of a Listening Glass. Touch this icon to open a new window with a list of audio recordings that other conference participants have contributed. You can also record your own audio for inclusion in the list.

The location of the Listening Glass serves as a gathering place - go there to meet and listen to the view out the window.
